Nostalgia

Written by Ella on 2026-07-01

it feels like in the past, there was more optimism regarding the future of the world. we had more information about things constantly improving, how life is getting better for everyone

now it seems like everything in the world is just slowly getting worse, with no real action to do anything about it or improve the situation that we are in

how much of this is me being more aware of the world around me, i cannot say

or how much of it is media influence with companies wanting people to be de-motivated so they can keep doing whatever they want

am i just looking back on the past with rose-tinted glasses because of my own prior (or current) ignorance?
